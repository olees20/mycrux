# Account deactivation and deletion workflow

Deactivation is immediate and reversible by reviewed support: active gym memberships become suspended and the profile is marked deactivated, while customer data is preserved. A deletion request is a separate tracked workflow. Support must verify identity, create the member export if requested, identify every tenant relationship and document the resolution.

Ordinary profile fields, private activity, notifications, social content and unattached media should be deleted or anonymised when the request is approved. The following may be retained only for the documented period and lawful basis: immutable waiver text and acceptance evidence; billing/tax records; fraud, abuse and safety investigations; competition integrity records; and append-only audit events. Retained records should replace display/contact data with a stable anonymous subject reference where the legal purpose permits. Auth identity deletion happens last, after tenant data processing and recovery-window expiry.

Marketing consent is independent of service notifications and community-guideline acceptance. Revoking marketing does not suppress authentication, booking, safety, waiver, billing or service-status messages. Owner subject-access tools must operate on one verified member at a time; bulk profile or waiver exports are not permitted through normal APIs.
